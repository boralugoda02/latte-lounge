Our Story

A proper stop on a long climb

The road between Beragala and Hali Ela is one of the most beautiful drives in the hill country — and one of the longest. Latte Lounge by 7's started as a simple idea: give travelers a warm, unhurried place to break the journey.

Set against the cool mountain air of Bandarawela, we serve coffee brewed strong enough for the climb ahead and snacks hearty enough to hold you until the next town. Come for five minutes or stay for the afternoon — the table by the window is usually free.
