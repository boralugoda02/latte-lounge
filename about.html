Our Story

Built for the road up the hills

The Beragala–Hali Ela highway climbs through some of the sharpest bends in the hill country, and by the time most travelers reach us, they've earned a proper stop. Latte Lounge by 7's opened as exactly that: a place to pull over, breathe the cool mountain air and warm up with something good.

We sit at a bend where the mist rolls in most afternoons, so the lounge was designed around that climate — big windows, warm light and hot drinks always on standby. Regulars know us for the coffee; travelers remember us for the quiet.

Everything on the menu is built around what actually grows nearby: estate coffee, garden vegetables and the kind of short eats you'd expect from a home kitchen in Uva, not a roadside stall.
